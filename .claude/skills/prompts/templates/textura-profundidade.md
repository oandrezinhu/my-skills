# Template: Imagem com Textura e Profundidade

## Quando usar
- Hero shots de produto
- Criativos de feed que precisam parar o scroll
- Campanhas que vendem qualidade, durabilidade, premium

## Estrutura do prompt

```
[sujeito/produto], [ação ou estado], extreme close-up macro photography,
[material texture: metal / rubber / leather / stone / fabric], 
high depth of field, bokeh background, [iluminação],
editorial product photography, hyper-realistic, 
[mood: dramatic / clean / raw / cinematic],
[paleta dominante], sharp micro-details, 
shot on Hasselblad, 100mm macro lens, f/2.8,
ultra detailed, 8k, studio quality
```

## Exemplos por segmento

### Produto industrial (peça/componente)
```
large industrial o-ring rubber seal, extreme close-up macro photography,
black rubber texture with micro surface detail, reflective metallic rim,
dramatic side lighting with rim light, dark navy background,
hyper-realistic, editorial product photography, cinematic mood,
shot on Hasselblad, 100mm macro lens, f/2.8, 8k, ultra sharp
```

### Produto alimentício
```
artisan coffee beans spilling on dark wood surface, extreme close-up macro photography,

shallow depth of field with warm bokeh, hyper-realistic,
editorial food photography, cozy premium mood,

```

### Cosmético / Skincare
```
skincare serum dropper with golden liquid drop falling, extreme close-up macro photography,
glass texture and golden fluid detail, clean white marble background,
softbox overhead lighting with subtle rim light, hyper-realistic,

```

## Negative prompt padrão
```
cartoon, illustration, flat, 3d render artificial, plastic look, overexposed,
washed out colors, motion blur, noise, grain, watermark, text
```

## Dicas de uso no Freepik
- Selecione estilo "Photography" ou "Realistic"
- Ative "High detail" se disponível
- Para produto com fundo limpo, adicione: "pure white/black background, product only"
